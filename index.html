Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't pass the NFET?

Your Starter Kit includes a complete NFET Preparation Guide covering all five sections of the exam. Results are communicated in the third week of April. The guide is designed to maximise your chances of clearing on the first attempt.

Why do I get the offer letter before training starts?

Because GCCs need job-ready talent that's matched to their specific roles — not generic candidates. GCC School's corporate partners commit to candidates early through the hiring drive. You train for an actual confirmed role, not a hypothetical one. This is the Employment-to-Education (E2E) model.

What happens after I pay the ₹2,950?

You'll receive an immediate WhatsApp confirmation + access to the preparation guide and dossier. A GCC School team member will reach out within 2 hours. Your NFET slot booking window is March 10 – April 15, 2026. The cohort starts July 1, 2026.

Is the ₹2,950 fee refundable?

The Starter Kit fee is non-refundable as it confirms your reservation with GCC School's hiring partners and allocates an NFET exam slot to you specifically.

What qualifications do I get at the end of 12 months?

You receive ACCA qualification progress (up to Strategic Professional level), a PGCM from AIMA (AICTE-approved), British Council English Certification, and the option to pursue an MSc in Professional Accountancy from University of London. All while holding a conditional job offer.

NFET Slot Booking Closes April 15. The Next Cohort Is Not Guaranteed.

GCC hiring partners commit to a fixed number of candidates per batch. Once the slot booking window closes, seats cannot be added. This is not artificial scarcity — it's how the programme is structured.
